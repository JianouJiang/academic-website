Parametric Assessment of Wind Farm Performance Under Varying Terrain and Atmospheric Stability Using an Analytical Wake Model 基于解析尾流模型的风电场性能在不同地形与大气稳定度下的参数化评估

Stability-driven differences in wind farm power are commonly attributed to wake recovery rates, but the relative contributions of hub-height wind speed and wake dynamics are rarely separated. We present a matched-velocity analysis showing that when hub-height wind speed is held constant, the residual stability effect on wake recovery accounts for only ~7% variation in farm power — while total power variation exceeds a factor of 2.3 when each condition uses its Monin-Obukhov prescribed wind speed. The framework uses the Bastankhah-Porté-Agel Gaussian wake model with stability- and terrain-dependent corrections, applied to a 3x3 NREL 5 MW farm across three terrain types and three stability classes. RANS validation in OpenFOAM confirms farm-level power agreement within 5.2%. 风电场功率的稳定度差异通常归因于尾流恢复速率，但轮毂高度风速与尾流动力学的相对贡献很少被分离。我们提出了匹配风速分析，表明当轮毂高度风速保持恒定时，稳定度对尾流恢复的残余效应仅占约 7% 的功率变化——而当各工况使用其 Monin-Obukhov 规定风速时，总功率变化超过 2.3 倍。该框架使用 Bastankhah-Porté-Agel 高斯尾流模型结合稳定度和地形修正，应用于三种地形和三种稳定度等级下的 3x3 NREL 5 MW 风场。OpenFOAM RANS 验证确认风场级功率一致性在 5.2% 以内。

Machine Learning Demand Forecasting Integrating Weather, Usage Patterns, and Pricing to Reduce Utility Generation Waste 融合天气、用电模式与电价的机器学习需求预测：降低电力系统发电浪费

We propose the Unified Multi-Source Ensemble (UMSE) framework, which fuses gradient-boosted trees (XGBoost) and LSTM networks through a learned attention mechanism to forecast short-term electricity demand. Evaluated on a dataset reproducing the statistical properties of a mid-sized North American utility, all models incorporating usage features achieve MAPE below 1%. An honest evaluation reveals that XGBoost alone outperforms the full ensemble — the attention mechanism assigns 96.3% weight to the tree branch. Both approaches reduce cumulative over-generation waste by approximately 77% relative to persistence baseline. 我们提出了统一多源集成（UMSE）框架，通过学习注意力机制融合梯度提升树（XGBoost）与 LSTM 网络，用于短期电力需求预测。在再现北美中型电力公司统计特性的数据集上评估，所有结合用电特征的模型均实现 MAPE 低于 1%。诚实评估表明，单独的 XGBoost 优于完整集成——注意力机制将 96.3% 的权重分配给树模型分支。两种方法均将累计过度发电浪费降低约 77%。

Vortex Rope Morphology Controls Runner Fatigue Failure Mode: An Analytical Framework Linking Draft Tube Flow Physics to Blade Life Prediction in Francis Turbines 涡带形态决定转轮疲劳失效模式：连接尾水管流场物理与 Francis 水轮机叶片寿命预测的分析框架

Francis turbines operating off-design develop distinct draft-tube vortex rope morphologies — helical at part-load, columnar at overload, and double-helix at deep part-load — each generating pressure pulsations with different circumferential phase distributions. We present an analytical framework that links these pressure phase patterns to mode-dependent runner stress and fatigue-life estimates using one-way FSI and rain-flow damage accumulation. The framework predicts three distinct failure mechanisms at different blade locations. Francis 水轮机在偏离设计工况运行时会产生不同的尾水管涡带形态——部分负荷下为螺旋形、过负荷下为柱状、深度部分负荷下为双螺旋——每种形态产生具有不同周向相位分布的压力脉动。我们提出了一个分析框架，利用单向流固耦合和雨流计数损伤累积，将压力相位模式与模态相关的转轮应力和疲劳寿命估算联系起来。该框架预测了叶片不同位置的三种不同失效机制。

Adaptive-Fidelity Hydrodynamic Load Calculation for Floating Offshore Wind Turbines: A Physics-Based Switching Framework 浮式海上风力发电机组自适应保真度水动力荷载计算：基于物理的切换框架

Mid-fidelity hydrodynamic models for floating offshore wind turbines rely on fixed Morison coefficients that do not capture nonlinear force variation across flow regimes. We present a diagnostic and correction framework that evaluates physics-based fidelity indicators — the Keulegan-Carpenter number, wave steepness, and relative motion amplitude — at each structural member and time step. Applied to the DeepCwind semi-submersible, the framework reveals that targeted corrections reduce surge in regular waves but amplify response by up to 278% in irregular seas, while unconditional correction triggers catastrophic resonant instability. 浮式海上风力发电机组的中等保真度水动力模型依赖固定的 Morison 系数，无法捕捉不同流动区域的非线性力变化。我们提出了一个诊断与修正框架，在每个结构构件和每个时间步评估基于物理的保真度指标——Keulegan-Carpenter 数、波陡和相对运动幅值。应用于 DeepCwind 半潜式平台，该框架揭示了定向修正在规则波中降低纵荡，但在不规则海况下将响应放大高达 278%，而无条件修正则触发灾难性共振失稳。

About关于

I am a PhD researcher in the Department of Engineering Science at the University of Oxford, supervised by Professor Budimir Rosic. My research sits at the intersection of computational fluid dynamics, machine learning, and structural mechanics — applied to energy systems ranging from hydropower turbines to floating offshore wind platforms and electricity grids. 我是牛津大学工程科学系的博士研究生，导师为 Budimir Rosic 教授。我的研究处于计算流体力学、机器学习与结构力学的交叉领域——应用于从水力发电涡轮机到浮式海上风电平台及电力系统等能源系统。

My work focuses on developing physics-informed computational frameworks that bridge multiple scales and fidelities: from vortex-resolved flow simulations in turbine draft tubes, to adaptive hydrodynamic load models for offshore structures, to ensemble machine learning for grid-scale demand forecasting. 我的工作专注于开发跨尺度、跨保真度的物理信息计算框架：从涡轮尾水管中的涡分辨流场模拟，到海上结构的自适应水动力荷载模型，再到电网规模需求预测的集成机器学习。
